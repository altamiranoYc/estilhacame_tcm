Destroy Me

Set between the events of Estilhaça-me and Liberta-me, this novel from the Estilhaça-me series offers a unprecedented perspective: the complex mind of Warner, the ruthless leader of Sector 45. After being injured by Juliette, the girl he tried to capture and control, Warner recovers while dealing with conflicting emotions. Obsessed with Juliette, he not only wants to find her again, but he also understands that his feelings for her go beyond what he imagined. At the same time, Warner faces increasing pressure from his father, the ruthless supreme commander of the Reestablishment, which forces him to make decisions that could define his and Juliette's future. "Destroy Me" reveals the deepest thoughts of a character who is both villain and victim, bringing a new perspective on the dystopian world and the complexities of the series Shatter me.
