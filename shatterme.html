Shatter Me

No one knows why Juliette's touch is fatal, but the Reestablishment, the regime that controls the world, considers this ability a danger and keeps it imprisoned. Alone in a cell for 264 days, Juliette lives isolated, without touching or being touched, marked by fear and guilt. When the regime decides to use her as a weapon to strengthen its power, Juliette must choose: whether to become a tool of destruction or struggle for your freedom. Next to Adam, a soldier who awakens feelings from the past, she discovers that her power may not just be a curse, but also the key to changing the world. In this first book in the series, Tahereh Mafi takes us through an emotional and visceral narrative, full of tension, romance and self-discovery as Juliette struggles to reclaim her place in a world that rejected her.
