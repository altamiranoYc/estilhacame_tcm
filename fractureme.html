Fracture Me

Told from the point of view of Adam Kent, this novel takes place parallel to the final events of Set Me Free and offers a deeper look into his role in the fight against the Reestablishment. As the Omega Point resistance prepares for its most dangerous confrontation, Adam is Torn between protecting his younger brother, James, and fighting alongside Juliette, the girl he loves. With the future uncertain and the pressures of war weighing on him, Adam begins to question his choices and the world you are trying to save. "Break Me Apart" explores Adam's emotions at a critical moment, offering a unique perspective about the final battle and the difficult decisions that will shape the characters' fates and the outcome from the Shatter Me series.
